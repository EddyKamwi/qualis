Our Story

Founded by Edward Kamwi, Qualis Designers emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between quality web design and programming education in Zambia. With years of experience in both development and teaching, we understand the unique challenges businesses face in the digital landscape.

Our mission is to empower businesses with professional web solutions while nurturing the next generation of developers through comprehensive programming education.
