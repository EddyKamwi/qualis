Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Web Services

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline varies depending on the project scope. A simple website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex projects may take 8-12 weeks or more.

Do you provide website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally.

What is your web development process?

Our process includes: Initial consultation, Planning & Design, Development, Testing, and Launch. We keep you involved throughout the entire process with regular updates and feedback sessions.

Do you offer hosting services?

Yes, we provide reliable hosting solutions with regular backups and security monitoring. We can also work with your existing hosting provider if preferred.

Programming Tutoring

What programming languages do you teach?

We cover popular languages including JavaScript, Python, PHP, and more. Our curriculum is tailored to your learning goals.

Are the tutoring sessions online or in-person?

We offer both online and in-person tutoring options to accommodate your preferences and schedule.

How long are the tutoring sessions?

Standard sessions are 1-2 hours long, but we can customize the duration based on your needs and learning pace.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for group sessions. Contact us for more information about our group pricing.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and major credit cards. For projects, we typically require a deposit upfront with milestone payments.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we can arrange flexible payment plans for larger projects. This will be discussed and agreed upon during the initial consultation.
